Why I Created Baudelaire Clinics

I founded Baudelaire Clinics out of a deep, personal need to unify fragmented skincare. People were being sent to different specialists with no one treating them as a whole. I kept thinking: why isn’t there one place where everything comes together—where science and beauty speak the same language?

Baudelaire Clinics is the answer—a space where cosmetic dermatology, evidence-based skincare, and aesthetic medicine merge into one philosophy: skin is more than surface. It’s biology, identity, and expression. We treat you, not trends.

Who Am I?

I grew up in Nice, began in pharmacy, and pursued a PhD in photobiology at the University of Bath. After clinical dermatology training at the Royal College of Physicians of Ireland and advanced aesthetics in London, I brought it all together at Baudelaire Clinics.

This clinic reflects my vision—to treat skin not just clinically or cosmetically, but intelligently, personally, and beautifully.
